PhD Candidate @ University of Naples Federico II -  itee  DESSERT

I am a PhD candidate at the University of Naples Federico II, enrolled in the Information Technologies and Electrical Engineering PhD Course. My PhD defense is planned for the beginning of 2018.
My research activity is on Software Reliability Engineering, with a deep focus on Android Operating System.
I am working on Android robustness testing and Android aging. My scientific interests widen to software engineering and distributed systems, but I am also looking forward to the Internet of Things (IoT).
I am tutored by prof. Domenico Cotroneo, and Roberto Natella. During my PhD course, I was a visiting scholar at Northeastern University (Boston, US) for the academic year 2016/2017, tutored by prof. Cristina Nita-Rotaru.

Prior to joining the PhD course, I obtained my B.Sc. (October 2011) and M.Sc. (January 2014) in Computer Science Engineering, both summa cum laude, from University of Naples Federico II.

I'm currently a contributor to the FЯIDA project: more precisely, to the low-level code instrumentation library frida-gum.
